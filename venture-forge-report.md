# Venture Forge Report

## Score: 64/100 — Promising but Risky

RoundtableCI presents an innovative AI orchestration platform that addresses the challenges of model performance evaluation. The technical team has demonstrated early traction with a live product and initial user engagement. However, the startup lacks clear market validation and a robust revenue model, which raises concerns about its growth potential. The competitive landscape further complicates its path to success. Overall, while the concept is compelling, significant refinement is needed to ensure sustainable traction.

## Investor Hook
Revolutionizing AI model selection with real-time performance insights.
